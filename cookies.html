Anti-Tracking & No Cookies Policy

Last updated: Sept 4, 2024 - 6:04PM

Snailairy.com has implemented a strict anti-tracking and no cookies policy to ensure that your browsing experience on our website is free from any tracking or unnecessary data collection.

1. No Cookies Policy

We do not use any cookies on our website. Cookies are small text files stored on your device that are often used by websites to track your activity and preferences.

  • No Analytics Cookies: We do not use any third-party analytics services (such as Google Analytics) that require cookies to track your visits or behavior on our site.
  • No Advertising Cookies: We do not serve ads on our site and do not use any advertising cookies to track or target you for advertising purposes.
  • No Functional Cookies: We do not use any functional or performance cookies that collect information to enhance your browsing experience. All necessary website functions are designed to work without the need for cookies.

2. No Tracking Technologies

We do not use any tracking technologies, including but not limited to:

  • Web Beacons: Small graphic images or scripts embedded in web pages or emails.
  • Pixel Tags: Invisible tags used to track behavior or collect data.
  • Fingerprinting: Techniques used to identify and track your device.

3. Data Collection

We do not collect any personal information about you while you are browsing our website, except for the country from which you are visiting. This information is collected solely for statistical purposes and to understand the geographic distribution of our visitors. It is not used to identify or track individual users.

4. Your Privacy Rights

We respect your right to privacy and strive to ensure that your online experience is secure and private. If you have any questions or concerns about our anti-tracking and no cookies policy, please feel free to contact us at sgmaer56@icloud.com.

Thank you for visiting Snailairy.com. Your privacy is important
